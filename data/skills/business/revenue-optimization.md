# Revenue Optimization

Grow revenue from existing customers through upsell, cross-sell, and retention strategies.

## Expansion Revenue Framework

### Upsell (Higher Tier)
- **Trigger signals**: Customer hitting usage limits, requesting gated features, growing team
- **Timing**: After customer achieves first value milestone (not during onboarding)
- **Approach**: Show usage report highlighting what they'd gain with upgrade
- **Target**: 20-30% of customers upgrade within first year

### Cross-Sell (Additional Products)
- **Identify adjacent needs**: What problem do they solve right after using your product?
- **Bundle pricing**: Discount cross-sold products 10-20% vs standalone
- **In-product prompts**: Surface cross-sell at moment of need (contextual, not annoying)
- **Target**: 15-25% of customers buy add-on within first year

### Usage-Based Expansion
- Price usage-based with overage charges or automatic tier bumps
- Growth in customer's business naturally grows your revenue
- Send proactive notifications: "You're at 80% of your plan limit"
- Offer annual commitment at current usage + 20% buffer for discount

## Retention Strategies

### Churn Prevention
1. **Identify at-risk signals**: declining usage, support tickets up, login frequency down
2. **Health score**: Combine product usage, support interactions, billing status into 0-100 score
3. **Intervention playbook**:
   - Score 70-85: automated nurture email with tips and unused features
   - Score 50-70: CSM outreach, offer training session
   - Score < 50: executive escalation, offer custom plan or discount
4. **Save offers**: When customer cancels, offer pause, downgrade, or limited-time discount

### Reducing Involuntary Churn
- Failed payment recovery: retry logic (day 1, 3, 7, 14) + dunning emails
- Card expiration pre-notification: email 30 and 7 days before expiry
- Offer multiple payment methods (card, ACH, wire for enterprise)
- Involuntary churn is often 20-40% of total churn — low-hanging fruit

## Pricing Levers

| Lever | Implementation | Revenue Impact |
|-------|---------------|----------------|
| Annual discount | 15-20% off for annual commit | Improved cash flow, lower churn |
| Seat-based pricing | Charge per user/seat | Grows with customer org |
| Feature gating | Key features in higher tiers | Drives upgrades |
| Professional services | Implementation, training, consulting | High-margin add-on |
| API/integration access | Premium tier for API access | Captures technical value |

## Measurement

### Key Revenue Metrics
- **Net Revenue Retention (NRR)**: Target > 110%
- **Expansion MRR as % of new MRR**: Target > 30% (healthy expansion machine)
- **Gross margin**: Track per product/service line
- **Revenue per employee**: Efficiency metric, benchmark against peers
- **Cohort revenue curves**: Should slope upward (expansion > churn within cohorts)

### Analysis Cadence
- Weekly: MRR waterfall, churn events, health score distribution
- Monthly: Cohort analysis, expansion by segment, at-risk account review
- Quarterly: Pricing review, competitive positioning, product-revenue alignment
