# Product Launch

Systematic approach to validating, launching, and scaling a product.

## MVP Validation

### Before Writing Code
1. **Problem validation**: Interview 20+ potential users — do they have this problem?
2. **Willingness to pay**: "Would you pay $X for this?" (only "yes" counts, "maybe" = no)
3. **Landing page test**: Build page describing the product, measure signup conversion (> 5% = signal)
4. **Concierge MVP**: Deliver the value manually for 5-10 customers before automating
5. **Smoke test**: Collect payment before building — pre-orders validate demand

### MVP Scope Rules
- Solve one problem completely rather than many problems partially
- Target one specific user persona (not "everyone")
- Time-box development to 2-4 weeks maximum
- If it takes longer, the scope is too big — cut features ruthlessly
- Ship when it's embarrassing but functional

## Beta Testing

### Structured Beta Program
1. **Recruit**: 20-50 beta users from your target persona (not friends and family)
2. **Onboard**: Personal onboarding call for each beta user (learn their workflow)
3. **Instrument**: Track every action — what they use, what they skip, where they drop off
4. **Feedback loops**: Weekly survey + monthly call with each beta user
5. **Iterate**: Ship updates weekly based on feedback, close the loop publicly

### Beta Success Criteria
- **Retention**: > 40% weekly active after 4 weeks
- **NPS**: > 30 from beta cohort
- **Activation**: > 60% complete core action within first session
- **Organic sharing**: > 10% of beta users refer someone unprompted

## Launch Checklist

### Pre-Launch (2 weeks before)
- [ ] Landing page optimized with clear value prop and CTA
- [ ] Payment flow tested end-to-end (Stripe, subscriptions, invoices)
- [ ] Onboarding flow tested with 5 new users (observe, don't guide)
- [ ] Support system ready (help docs, email support, chat if applicable)
- [ ] Analytics tracking verified (signup, activation, conversion, revenue)

### Launch Day
- [ ] Product Hunt submission scheduled (Tuesday-Thursday, 12:01 AM PT)
- [ ] Social media announcements queued
- [ ] Email to waitlist and beta users
- [ ] Hacker News "Show HN" post prepared
- [ ] Monitoring dashboards for errors, load, conversion

### Post-Launch (first 2 weeks)
- [ ] Respond to every comment and review within 2 hours
- [ ] Daily metrics review: signups, activation, conversion, churn
- [ ] Collect and act on user feedback immediately
- [ ] Fix critical bugs same day, improvements within the week
- [ ] Share launch results and learnings publicly (builds audience)

## Go-to-Market Motions

| Motion | Best For | CAC | Time to Revenue |
|--------|---------|-----|-----------------|
| Product-led growth | Self-serve, low price | Low ($5-50) | 1-3 months |
| Sales-led | Enterprise, complex | High ($500-5K) | 3-6 months |
| Community-led | Developer tools, niche | Low ($10-100) | 3-6 months |
| Content-led | Educational products | Medium ($50-200) | 6-12 months |
