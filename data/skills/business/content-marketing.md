# Content Marketing

Create and distribute content that drives organic traffic, builds authority, and generates leads.

## SEO Content Strategy

### Keyword Research Process
1. **Seed keywords**: List 10-20 topics your ideal customer searches for
2. **Expand**: Use Ahrefs, SEMrush, or free alternatives (Google autocomplete, AlsoAsked)
3. **Filter**: Target keywords with volume > 100/mo, difficulty < 40 (new sites), commercial intent
4. **Cluster**: Group related keywords into topic clusters around pillar pages
5. **Prioritize**: Bottom-of-funnel first (buyer intent), then mid-funnel (consideration), then top (awareness)

### Content Types by Funnel Stage
| Stage | Content Type | Keyword Pattern | Goal |
|-------|-------------|----------------|------|
| Bottom | Comparison, pricing, reviews | "X vs Y", "best X for Y" | Convert to trial/demo |
| Middle | How-to guides, tutorials | "how to solve X" | Capture email, build trust |
| Top | Industry trends, opinion | "what is X", "why X matters" | Brand awareness |

## Content Production

### Article Structure (SEO-Optimized)
1. **Title**: Include primary keyword, under 60 characters
2. **Intro** (100 words): State the problem, promise the solution
3. **Body**: H2/H3 headings with secondary keywords, actionable steps
4. **Visuals**: Screenshots, diagrams, tables every 300-500 words
5. **CTA**: Relevant next step (sign up, download, read more)
6. **Meta description**: 150-160 characters, includes keyword and value prop

### Production Cadence
- Minimum: 2 high-quality articles per week for new sites
- Established sites: 4-8 articles per week
- Update existing top-performing content quarterly (refresh stats, add sections)
- One pillar page per topic cluster (2,000-4,000 words), supported by 5-10 cluster articles

## Distribution

### Channels
- **Organic search**: Primary long-term driver (takes 3-6 months to build)
- **Social media**: Share on LinkedIn, Twitter/X, relevant subreddits (drive initial traffic)
- **Email newsletter**: Weekly digest to subscriber list (highest engagement)
- **Content syndication**: Republish on Medium, Dev.to, HackerNoon with canonical link
- **Community seeding**: Share in Slack/Discord communities where your ICP hangs out

### Amplification Tactics
- Mention and notify influencers/companies cited in the article
- Create derivative content: thread from article, infographic, short video
- Submit to aggregators: Hacker News, Product Hunt, niche forums

## Measurement

### Key Metrics
| Metric | Target | Frequency |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Organic traffic | +10% MoM | Weekly |
| Keyword rankings (page 1) | +5 new/month | Weekly |
| Email subscribers from content | 2-5% conversion | Monthly |
| Content → MQL conversion | Track attribution | Monthly |
| Time on page | > 3 minutes | Monthly |

### Attribution
- Use UTM parameters on all distributed links
- Track first-touch and last-touch attribution to content pieces
- Identify which content pieces influence pipeline (multi-touch attribution)
