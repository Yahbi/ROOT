# Lead Generation

Build a systematic pipeline for finding, qualifying, and nurturing potential customers.

## Outbound Prospecting

### Building Target Lists
1. **Define ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)**: industry, company size, tech stack, budget, pain points
2. **Source contacts**: LinkedIn Sales Navigator, Apollo.io, ZoomInfo, Clearbit
3. **Enrich data**: Append email, phone, company info, tech stack, recent funding
4. **Verify emails**: Use NeverBounce, ZeroBounce, or Hunter.io to reduce bounce rate to < 3%
5. **Segment**: Group by priority (Tier 1: perfect fit, Tier 2: good fit, Tier 3: possible fit)

### Outreach Sequences
| Channel | Cadence | Purpose |
|---------|---------|---------|
| Cold email | 5-7 emails over 3 weeks | Primary outreach, scalable |
| LinkedIn | 3-4 touches over 2 weeks | Warm up before/after email |
| Cold call | 2-3 attempts per week | Highest conversion per touch |

### Email Sequence Template
- **Email 1**: Short pain-point hook + value prop + soft CTA (reply if interested)
- **Email 2** (day 3): Case study or social proof relevant to their industry
- **Email 3** (day 7): Different angle on the problem, mention competitor
- **Email 4** (day 14): Breakup email — last follow-up, recap value
- Keep each email under 100 words. Personalize first line to their company/role.

## Inbound Lead Generation

### Content-Driven Pipeline
1. **SEO content**: Target bottom-of-funnel keywords ("best X for Y", "X vs Y", "how to solve Z")
2. **Lead magnets**: Templates, calculators, reports gated behind email capture
3. **Webinars**: Educational content → email list → nurture to demo
4. **Community**: Discord/Slack group builds trust → organic conversion

### Conversion Optimization
- Landing page → CTA above the fold, single clear action
- Form fields: fewer is better (name + email for top-of-funnel, add company + role for demos)
- Social proof: logos, testimonials, case study snippets near the CTA
- A/B test headlines, CTAs, and form length continuously

## Lead Qualification

### Scoring Framework (BANT)
| Criteria | Score 1-5 | Weight |
|----------|----------|--------|
| **B**udget: Can they afford it? | | 25% |
| **A**uthority: Is this the decision maker? | | 25% |
| **N**eed: Do they have the problem you solve? | | 30% |
| **T**iming: Are they buying now? | | 20% |

### Lead Stages
- **MQL (Marketing Qualified)**: Engaged with content, downloaded asset, visited pricing page
- **SQL (Sales Qualified)**: Confirmed budget, need, and timeline through conversation
- **Opportunity**: Demo completed, proposal sent, in evaluation

## Metrics to Track

- Lead volume by source (outbound, inbound organic, inbound paid)
- Email open rate (target > 40%), reply rate (target > 5% cold)
- MQL → SQL conversion rate (target > 20%)
- SQL → Closed Won rate (target > 25%)
- Cost per lead by channel, cost per SQL, cost per closed deal
