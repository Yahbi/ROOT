# Revenue Modeling

## Purpose
Model SaaS/product revenue: TAM, unit economics, MRR projections, and sustainability analysis.

## Steps
1. Define the total addressable market (TAM) by identifying target customers, willingness to pay, and market size
2. Build unit economics: customer acquisition cost (CAC), average revenue per user (ARPU), and lifetime value (LTV)
3. Model monthly recurring revenue (MRR) growth using realistic assumptions for acquisition rate and churn
4. Project cash flow over 12, 24, and 36 months including infrastructure costs, payroll, and marketing spend
5. Calculate key health ratios: LTV/CAC (target > 3), payback period (target < 12 months), and gross margin
6. Run sensitivity analysis by varying churn rate, conversion rate, and pricing to find break-even thresholds
7. Identify the path to sustainability: what MRR level covers all costs and how many months to reach it

## Output Format
A revenue model with monthly projections table, unit economics summary, sensitivity ranges, and a timeline to profitability.
