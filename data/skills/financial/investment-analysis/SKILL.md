# Investment Analysis

## Purpose
Analyze investment opportunities: ROI, risk-adjusted returns, payback period, and comparison to alternatives.

## Steps
1. Define the total investment required including cash outlay, time cost valued at opportunity rate, and hidden costs
2. Project expected returns over 1, 3, and 5 year horizons using conservative, base, and optimistic scenarios
3. Calculate ROI, internal rate of return (IRR), and payback period for the base case
4. Apply risk adjustment by discounting returns based on probability of achieving projected outcomes
5. Compare against alternatives including index funds, other projects, and doing nothing
6. Assess liquidity: how easily can the investment be exited and what are the lock-up constraints

## Output Format
An investment memo with returns projections, risk-adjusted metrics, comparison table against alternatives, and a clear invest or pass recommendation.
