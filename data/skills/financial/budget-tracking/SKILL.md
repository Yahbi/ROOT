# Budget Tracking

## Purpose
Set up and maintain budget tracking to monitor income, expenses, and spending patterns, providing clear visibility into financial health and progress toward savings goals.

## Steps
1. Define budget categories (fixed costs, variable costs, savings, discretionary)
2. Set budget limits for each category based on income and financial goals
3. Record all income sources and their expected amounts and timing
4. Log expenses with date, amount, category, and description
5. Calculate running totals and compare actual spending against budget limits
6. Identify overspending categories and areas for optimization
7. Generate a summary report with variance analysis and trend visualization

## Output Format
A budget tracking sheet or report with categorized income and expenses, budget vs. actual comparisons, variance highlights, and recommendations for adjustments.
