# DeFi Basics

Understand decentralized finance mechanics for yield generation and protocol evaluation.

## DEX Mechanics

### Automated Market Makers (AMMs)
- **Constant product formula**: x * y = k (Uniswap v2)
- Price is determined by the ratio of assets in the pool, not an order book
- Trades shift the ratio — larger trades cause more slippage
- **Concentrated liquidity** (Uniswap v3): LPs choose price ranges, higher capital efficiency

### Key DEX Metrics
| Metric | What It Tells You |
|--------|-------------------|
| TVL (Total Value Locked) | Protocol adoption and trust level |
| 24h Volume | Trading activity and fee generation |
| Volume/TVL ratio | Capital efficiency — higher is better |
| Fee tier | Revenue to LPs (0.01%, 0.05%, 0.3%, 1%) |

## Liquidity Provision

### How LP Positions Work
1. Deposit equal value of two tokens (e.g., $500 ETH + $500 USDC)
2. Receive LP tokens representing your share of the pool
3. Earn trading fees proportional to your pool share
4. Withdraw anytime — receive both tokens at current ratio

### Impermanent Loss
- Occurs when token prices diverge from your entry ratio
- 2x price change = ~5.7% IL, 5x = ~25.5% IL
- Only "permanent" if you withdraw during divergence
- IL must be offset by fee income for the position to be profitable
- Stablecoin pairs (USDC/USDT) have minimal IL — lower fees but safer

## Yield Farming

### Yield Sources
1. **Trading fees**: Real yield from protocol usage (sustainable)
2. **Token emissions**: Protocol rewards in governance tokens (dilutive, often unsustainable)
3. **Lending interest**: Supply assets to lending protocols (Aave, Compound)
4. **Staking rewards**: Proof-of-stake validator/delegator rewards

### Evaluating Yield
- **Real yield** = fees generated by actual protocol usage
- **Emission yield** = token rewards that dilute supply — check token inflation schedule
- APY > 100% is almost always emission-driven and temporary
- Compare yield to IL risk for LP positions — net yield is what matters

## Risk Assessment Checklist

- [ ] Smart contract audited by reputable firm (Trail of Bits, OpenZeppelin)
- [ ] Protocol live for > 6 months without exploit
- [ ] TVL > $10M (sufficient liquidity, lower rug risk)
- [ ] Team doxxed or backed by known investors
- [ ] Token emissions schedule is transparent and declining
- [ ] No admin keys that can drain funds (or behind timelock + multisig)
