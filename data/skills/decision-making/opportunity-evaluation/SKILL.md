# Opportunity Evaluation

## Purpose
Evaluate business opportunities through financial impact, time cost, risk, and goal alignment.

## Steps
1. Quantify the financial impact by estimating potential revenue, cost savings, or equity value over 12-36 months
2. Estimate the total time investment required including ramp-up, execution, and ongoing maintenance
3. Assess risk by mapping probability of failure against financial and reputational impact
4. Check alignment with Yohan's current goals, priorities, and strategic direction
5. Identify any deal breakers such as cash requirements, exclusivity clauses, or timeline conflicts
6. Calculate expected ROI and payback period using conservative, base, and optimistic scenarios
7. Make a clear go/no-go recommendation with the top three reasons supporting the decision

## Output Format
A structured evaluation with financial projections, risk matrix, alignment score, and a single recommendation with supporting rationale.
