# Risk Assessment

## Purpose
Systematic risk analysis with probability, impact, and mitigations for any decision or project.

## Steps
1. Identify all material risks by category: financial, operational, reputational, legal, and technical
2. Rate each risk on probability (1-5) and impact (1-5) to produce a severity score
3. Map risks on a probability-impact matrix to visualize the overall risk landscape
4. Develop specific mitigation strategies for every risk scoring above threshold (severity > 9)
5. Identify leading indicators that would signal each risk is materializing
6. Define contingency plans and exit criteria if key risks are triggered
7. Summarize the overall risk profile with a clear accept, mitigate, or reject recommendation

## Output Format
A risk register table with probability, impact, severity scores, mitigations, and an overall risk summary with recommended action.
