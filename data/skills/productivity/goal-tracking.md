# Goal Tracking

## Purpose
Define clear goals with measurable milestones, track progress over time, and maintain accountability to ensure consistent forward movement toward objectives.

## Steps
1. Define the goal using SMART criteria (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound)
2. Break the goal into milestones with target dates and success criteria
3. Identify key actions and habits required to reach each milestone
4. Set up a tracking system (checklist, spreadsheet, or project board)
5. Schedule regular check-ins to review progress and update status
6. Identify blockers early and adjust timelines or approach as needed
7. Celebrate milestone completions and capture lessons learned

## Output Format
A goal tracking document with the defined goal, milestone breakdown with dates and criteria, current progress status, blockers, and next actions.
