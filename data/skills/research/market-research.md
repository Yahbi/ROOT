# Market Research

## Purpose
Conduct structured market research to understand market size, target segments, competitive landscape, and trends that inform strategic decisions and product positioning.

## Steps
1. Define the research objective and key questions to answer
2. Identify the target market segments and their characteristics
3. Estimate market size (TAM, SAM, SOM) using top-down and bottom-up approaches
4. Map the competitive landscape (direct competitors, substitutes, market positioning)
5. Analyze industry trends, growth drivers, and potential disruptions
6. Gather primary data (surveys, interviews) or secondary data (reports, public filings)
7. Synthesize findings into actionable insights and recommendations

## Output Format
A market research report with market sizing estimates, competitive analysis matrix, trend summary, key insights, and strategic recommendations with supporting data sources cited.
