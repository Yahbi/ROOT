# Market Research

## When to Use
When Yohan needs market intelligence for business decisions.

## Procedure
1. Define Scope: Industry, geography, time period
2. Identify Sources: Reports, filings, news, social media
3. Competitive Landscape: Map competitors by size, feature, price
4. Market Sizing: TAM, SAM, SOM
5. Trend Analysis: Growth rates, emerging tech, shifts
6. Synthesize: Findings, opportunities, threats, recommendations
