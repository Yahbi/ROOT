# Email Drafting

## When to Use
When Yohan needs ROOT to draft professional emails.

## Templates
### Cold Outreach
- Hook: Reference something specific about their company
- Value: What you offer and why it matters
- Proof: Brief social proof or result
- CTA: Single clear ask

### Follow-Up
- Reference previous interaction
- Add new value (insight, resource, update)
- Keep it short (3-5 sentences)

## Rules
- Subject line under 50 chars, specific not generic
- First sentence must hook attention
- One CTA per email
- Proofread for tone (professional but human)
