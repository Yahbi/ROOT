# Pitch Deck Creation

## When to Use
When Yohan needs a pitch deck, investor presentation, or business proposal.

## Standard Structure (10 slides)
1. Title: Company name, tagline, contact
2. Problem: Pain point (make it emotional)
3. Solution: Your product/service
4. Market Size: TAM/SAM/SOM
5. Business Model: How you make money
6. Traction: Metrics, growth, customers
7. Competition: Positioning matrix
8. Team: Key people and their strengths
9. Financials: Revenue projections, unit economics
10. Ask: How much, what for, what milestones

## Rules
- One idea per slide
- Data beats words
- Tell a story (problem to solution to proof to ask)
