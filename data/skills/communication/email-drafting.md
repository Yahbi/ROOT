# Email Drafting

## Purpose
Draft clear, professional emails tailored to the audience and purpose, whether for outreach, follow-ups, requests, updates, or negotiations.

## Steps
1. Identify the recipient, relationship context, and communication goal
2. Choose the appropriate tone (formal, semi-formal, friendly professional)
3. Write a concise, specific subject line that signals the email's purpose
4. Open with context or a reference point the recipient will recognize
5. State the main message or request clearly in the body (one idea per paragraph)
6. Include a specific call to action with any deadlines or next steps
7. Close with an appropriate sign-off and review for clarity and brevity

## Output Format
A complete email draft with subject line, greeting, body paragraphs, call to action, and sign-off, ready to send or customize further.
